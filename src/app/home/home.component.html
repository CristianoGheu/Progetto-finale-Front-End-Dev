Climate Change Dashboard

Global warming is changing our planet day by day. This website lets you explore key environmental data to better understand the impact of greenhouse gases and climate change. You can view the trends in global temperatures, CO₂ levels, methane, nitrous oxide, and the ongoing reduction of Arctic ice. Looking at the data is the first step toward understanding reality and building a more informed future.

Temperature

Shows the rise of global temperatures since 1880 due to greenhouse gases.

CO2

Tracks atmospheric CO₂ levels over time, a major driver of climate change.

Methane

Displays methane (CH₄) concentration trends, a potent greenhouse gas.

NO2

Visualizes nitrous oxide levels, contributing to the greenhouse effect.

Polar Ice

Highlights Arctic sea ice decline, one of the most visible effects of warming.
